The Samoa Chamber of Commerce and Industry Incorporated is Samoa’s National Private Sector Organization (NPSO). It is an incorporated association of businesses and private sector organisations representing all areas of Samoa’s private sector.

The Samoa Chamber of Commerce (SCCI) is the recognized National Private Sector Organization of Samoa that promotes the interest and growth of Samoa's private sector at the national, regional and international levels to improve the economic and social wellbeing of the people of Samoa. Our work focuses on advocating the interests of our members and the business community by formulating and effectively promoting policies which encourages the development of the private sector.
